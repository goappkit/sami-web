# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (the "App") handles information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information Collection

The App operates primarily offline and does **not** collect or store personal information from users.

Documents scanned or created using the App are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

---

## 2. Camera and Photo Library

The App requires access to your device’s camera and photo library for the following purposes:

* **Camera**: To capture documents for scanning.
* **Photo Library**: To save scanned documents or import existing images for scanning.

The App does **not** access your camera or photos for any other purpose.

---

## 3. Local Data Storage

The App may store certain data locally to provide functionality, including:

* Scanned documents
* App settings
* Usage points or feature access data

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

---

## 4. Advertisements

The App may display third-party advertisements. These may collect non-personal information such as device info and interaction with ads.

Refer to the privacy policies of the respective advertising providers for details.

---

## 5. In-App Purchases

Optional in-app purchases may be offered. Payments are processed by the platform (Apple/Google). The developer does not store payment information.

The platform provider may collect information necessary to process the transaction according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 6. Data Security

Users are responsible for securing their devices and local data.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

---

## 8. Changes and Contact

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be published within the App or related website.

Questions can be directed to the developer via the contact info provided in the App.
