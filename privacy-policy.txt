# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 12, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how this application (the "App") handles information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information Collection

The App operates primarily offline. Documents scanned or created using the App are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

The App does not collect personal information on its own initiative. However, certain App features and third-party services integrated into the App may collect limited data as described in the sections below.

---

## 2. Camera and Photo Library

The App requires access to your device's camera and photo library for the following purposes:

* **Camera**: To capture documents for scanning.
* **Photo Library**: To save scanned documents or import existing images for scanning.

The App does **not** access your camera or photos for any other purpose.

---

## 3. Local Data Storage

The App stores certain data locally on your device to provide its functionality, including:

* Scanned documents
* App settings
* Usage points or feature access data

This data is not transmitted to the developer except when you voluntarily submit feedback (see Section 5).

---

## 4. Crash Reporting

The App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** (provided by Google) to automatically collect crash and error reports. This helps the developer identify and fix stability issues.

Data collected may include:

* Device model and operating system version
* App version
* Crash stack traces
* A Firebase installation identifier (not linked to your identity)

This data is sent to Google's servers and is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## 5. User Feedback

The App provides an optional feedback feature. When you submit feedback, the following information is sent to the developer via Google Forms:

* Feedback title and content (entered by you)
* Your email address (optional, only if you choose to provide it, solely for the developer to reply to you)
* Device operating system type and version (automatically attached to help diagnose issues)

Feedback data is stored on Google's servers and governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). Your email address will not be used for any purpose other than responding to your feedback.

---

## 6. Advertisements

The App may display third-party advertisements powered by **Google AdMob**. The advertising SDK may collect non-personal information such as device identifiers, ad interaction data, and (with your permission) advertising identifiers for ad personalization.

On iOS, the App will request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your advertising identifier. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. Your choice does not affect the core functionality of the App.

Refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how AdMob handles data.

---

## 7. In-App Purchases

Optional in-app purchases may be offered. Payments are processed by the platform (Apple/Google). The developer does not store payment information.

The platform provider may collect information necessary to process the transaction according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 8. Data Security

The developer takes reasonable steps to protect data submitted through the App. However, users are also responsible for securing their devices and local data.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

---

## 10. Changes and Contact

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be published within the App or on the related website.

For questions or concerns, contact the developer at: **support.soloscan@goappkit.com**
